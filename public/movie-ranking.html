MY TOP 3 MOVIES OF ALL TIME!

Not according to popular opinion...

1. Tropic Thunder
2. Austin Powers
3. Ace Ventura

1. Tropic Thunder

Consisting of one of the most highly-rated casts of all time, with actors such as Jack Black, Robert Downey Jr. and Ben Stiller, the comedic chaos that ensues will grip you from start to finish.

2. Austin Powers

Mike Myers is an absolute delight with his take on what a British Super Spy from the 80s looks like. Yeah baby!

3. Ace Ventura

We all know and love Jim Carrey! This was one of his most outstanding (and outlandish) roles to date. A true comedic genius of our era.
